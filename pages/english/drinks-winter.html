Winter Drinks

Wassail

Amount:

  • 8 Servings

Time:

  • 30 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup - sugar
  • 1/2 cup - brown sugar, packed
  • 4 cups - apple cider
  • 1 - cinnamon stick, 3 inches long
  • 12 - cloves whole
  • 2 cups - grapefruit juice
  • 2 cups - orange juice
  • 1 - orange sliced
  • 6 - maraschino cherries, halved

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan combine the sugar, brown sugar, and apple cider.
  2. Heat and stir until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Add the cinnamon sticks and cloves.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the grapefruit and orange juice.
  7. Heat but do not boil.
  8. Strain and pour the brew into a heat-proof punch bowl.
  9. Garnish with orange slices and maraschino cherries and serve!

    10. Optional:

    • Add mead at a 1:1 ratio for a delicious alcoholic version.
    • Add a shot of cherry liqueur to each serving.
    • Add a shot of honey whiskey or brandy to each serving.
